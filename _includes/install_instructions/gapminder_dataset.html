Gapminder dataset

To follow some of the demonstrations and exercises, you need a copy of the Gapminder dataset (from the Gapminder Foundation).

  1. Download the file python-novice-gapminder-data.zip and move it to your desktop.
  2. Unzip the file.
  3. The procedure varies depending on your operating system and browser. You should end up with a folder called "python-novice-gapminder-data" on your desktop, which contains a folder called "data". It may look similar to this on your computer, but depends on your OS, etc.:
